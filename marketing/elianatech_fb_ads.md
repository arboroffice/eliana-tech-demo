# Elianatech FB Ads: The AI Wing Suite

This document outlines 5 Facebook ad concepts for **Elianatech**, categorized by Eugene Schwartz's 5 Levels of Awareness. These ads are designed to move high-revenue business owners from "overworked founder" to "autonomous operator" by positioning Elianatech as their **AI Wing**.

---

## 🏗️ Stage 1: Unaware
*The prospect is successful but exhausted. They think 'hiring more people' is the only way to scale.*

### Ad 1: The CEO Bottleneck
**Headline:** You built a business. Then it trapped you.
**Body Copy:** You're the first one in and the last one out. Every decision routes through you. Every lead is on your shoulders. You didn't start this to become the most overworked person in the building. 
Stop hiring headcount to solve technical chaos. You don't need a COO—you need an AI Wing.
