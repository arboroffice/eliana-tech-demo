## Challenge

GadgetHub, a major online electronics retailer, faced a massive surge in customer inquiries during peak shopping seasons. Their human support team was overwhelmed, leading to 24-hour response delays and frustrated customers. Returns and technical questions were particularly bottlenecked.

## Solution

We deployed a specialized Customer Service AI Agent capable of handling complex technical queries.

*   **Instant Troubleshooting:** The AI guided users through device setup and troubleshooting steps in real-time.
*   **Automated Returns Processing:** Integrated with their logistics system to approve and label returns instantly based on policy.
*   **Semantic Search:** Allowed users to find products using natural language queries like 'best headphones for running'.
