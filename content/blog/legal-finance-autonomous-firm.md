## The Professional Services Ceiling: The Documentation Death-Spiral

The growth of a law or finance firm is usually vertical: more clients = more paperwork = more people needed to handle the paperwork. 
1.  **Intake Friction:** Discovery calls and massive questionnaires.
2.  **Compliance Lag:** KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML checks.

If you scale this model with humans, your margins actually *shrink* because of the management overhead and human error rates.
