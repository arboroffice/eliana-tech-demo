## The Churn Paradox: Why Members Leave

Members don't leave because of the price; they leave because they feel lost. In a large community, it's impossible for a human founder to notice every member who has stopped logging in or hasn't introduced themselves.

Our **Backend Admin** system acts as a "Retention Engine," monitoring member sentiment and activity levels in real-time.

### 1. The 24/7 Knowledge Concierge
Your community is a goldmine of information, but it's often buried in thousands of old threads and Discord messages. We build a "Searchable Brain" for your community. ### 2. Automated Onboarding & Engagement
The first 7 days are critical for member retention. Our AI agents guide every new member through a personalized onboarding flow based on their goals.
