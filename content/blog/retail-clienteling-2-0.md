## What is AI Clienteling?

It's a proactive, conversational agent that acts as a personal stylist or shopper.
*   **The Follow-up**: "Hi Sarah, how are those running shoes fitting? We just got a matching jacket in stock."
*   **The Consultation**: "I see you're looking for a gift for your husband. What's his style?" -> *AI generates 3 curated options.*

## Moving Beyond "Blast" SMS

Most SMS marketing is spam. "20% OFF EVERYTHING."
AI Clienteling is service. "Hi Mike, noticed you bought the coffee maker last month. Do you need a refill on filters?"

This shifts the dynamic from 'marketing' to 'helpful assistance', skyrocketing open rates and conversion.
