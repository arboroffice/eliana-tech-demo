## Layer 2: Self-Healing Field Operations

Your techs are great at fixing units, but they are usually bad at "Paperwork." 

**The Autonomous Field Layer:**
By connecting your field management app with an AI orchestration layer, the system manages the "Ground Truth" of the job.

- **The "Voice-to-Invoice" Bridge:** The tech records a 30-second voice memo: "Finished the repair. Replaced the capacitor, cleaned the coils. Unit is drawing 12 amps now." The AI instantly turns this into a professional, itemized invoice and drafts the "Service Summary" for the client.
- **Auto-Parts Procurement:** If the AI detects "Part Required" in the tech's notes, it automatically checks your inventory, identifies it’s out of stock, and drafts the PO for your supplier.
- **Proactive Denials & Upsells:** If the tech mentions "Old Unit" or "Upcoming Failure," the AI automatically drafts a "System Replacement Quote" and sends it to the client with a 0% financing offer—instantly.
