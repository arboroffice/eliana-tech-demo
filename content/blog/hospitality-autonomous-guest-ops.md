## Layer 2: Self-Healing Logistics & Cleaning

Your cleaning team is the heartbeat of your business. If they miss a beat, your reputation dies.

**The Autonomous Logistics Layer:**
By connecting your booking calendar with an AI orchestration layer, the system manages the "Ground Truth" of the property.

- **The "Auto-Dispatch" Cleaner:** The moment a booking checkout occurs, the AI pings the cleaning crew: "Unit 4 is ready for turnover. Check-in is at 3 PM today."
- **Visual Verification:** The cleaner sends photos of the finished unit to the AI. The system scans the photos for "Brand Standards" (e.g., Are the towels folded correctly? Is the fridge empty?) before marking the unit as "Ready."
- **Maintenance Awareness:** If a cleaner notes "Lightbulb out," the AI automatically drafts a task for the handyman and orders the part—without you ever being involved.
