You're on a job site. The saw is running, the crew has questions, and your phone won't stop vibrating in your pocket. It's a potential lead calling for a $50k kitchen remodel. You can't answer. By the time you call them back at 6 PM, they've already booked two other quotes with competitors who answered faster.

In the trades, **speed to lead is everything.** But as a founder, you can't be in two places at once. You can't be the master craftsman and the full-time intake coordinator.

This is where the **AI Foreman** comes in. A custom AI system doesn't just "chat"—it triages leads, qualifies budgets, schedules site visits, and keeps clients updated on project progress without you ever touching your phone. Let's build the blueprint for your automated trade business.

## Why Most Trade Businesses Hit a Ceiling

Most construction and trade companies follow the same trajectory: you're great at the work, so you get more work. You hire a crew. Now you're managing people *and* doing the sales. You hit a ceiling because you are the bottleneck for every single quote and every single customer update.

### The Leak: The "I'll Call You Back" Trap
If you take more than 15 minutes to respond to an inquiry, your chances of closing that lead drop by 80%. In a world of instant gratification, "I'll call you back" is a death sentence for your growth.

### The Waste: Driving for Quotes
How many hours a week do you spend driving 45 minutes to a "hot lead" only to find out they have a $5,000 budget for a $20,000 project? That’s wasted fuel, wasted time, and wasted opportunity.

### The Friction: The "Where Are We At?" Text
Clients are anxious. They want to know when the tile is arriving or why the plumber didn't show up at 8 AM. If you're the only one who can answer that, you're not a founder—you're a high-paid receptionist.

## Step 1: The 24/7 AI Intake Engine

Your AI needs to live where your customers are: your website, Google Business Profile, and Facebook/Instagram DMs.

### The Triage Flow:
When a lead reaches out, the AI shouldn't just say "Thanks, we'll call you." It needs to act like a trained office manager:

1.  **Identify the Project:** "What are we looking to build? A deck, a full renovation, or a repair?"
2.  **Qualify the Budget:** "Our average kitchen projects start at $15k. Is that within the range you're looking to invest?"
3.  **Check the Timeline:** "We're currently booking for April. Does that work for your schedule?"
4.  **Gather the Data:** "Can you upload 2-3 photos of the current space so our estimator can take a look?"

**The Result:** You only see the leads that are qualified, in-budget, and have already provided the photos you need to give a ballpark estimate.

## Step 2: Automated Site Visit Scheduling

Once a lead is qualified, the AI moves to the next stage of the funnel: **The Quote.**

Instead of the back-and-forth "Does Tuesday work? No, how about Wednesday?", the AI coordinates with your digital calendar (Google, Outlook, or Jobber).

- **Logic-Based Booking:** The AI knows you only do quotes on Tuesday/Thursday mornings. It knows you need 30 minutes of drive time between appointments.
- **Deposit Collection:** For high-demand trades, the AI can even collect a "Drafting Fee" or "Site Visit Deposit" before the appointment is confirmed.
- **Confirmation Sequences:** The AI sends a calendar invite, a text confirmation 24 hours before, and a "I'm on my way" text 30 minutes prior.

## Step 3: The Project Update Stream

This is where you win on reputation. Most contractors disappear once the deposit is paid. You're going to use AI to lead the industry in communication.

### The "Auto-Update" System:
By integrating your field management software (like Jobber or Procore) with an AI communication layer, you can automate the entire client experience:

- **Daily Summaries:** "Hey [Name], the crew finished the demo today. Tomorrow we start the rough-in plumbing. Here are 2 photos of today's progress."
- **Delay Management:** "We had a material delay on the cabinets. We're pushing the install to Friday. Sorry for the shift, we're on top of it."
- **Review Generation:** The moment a job is marked "Complete," the AI sends a personalized text: "We loved working on your home. Would you mind leaving us a quick review on Google? It helps our small team a ton."

## Step 4: Connecting the Field to the Office

Your AI Foreman needs access to the "Ground Truth" of your business. This requires a modern tech stack.

### The Recommended Trade Stack:
- **Lead Capture:** Custom AI agent on Website/SMS (via Eliana Tech)
