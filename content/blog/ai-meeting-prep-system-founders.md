## What an AI Meeting Prep System Does

An AI meeting preparation system automatically delivers a comprehensive briefing before every meeting on your calendar:

### The Briefing Document Includes:

**Company Overview:**
- What the company does
- Revenue/size estimate
- Industry and market position
- Recent news (funding, hiring, launches, press)
- Key products or services

**Person Overview:**
- Name, title, and role
- LinkedIn profile summary
- Recent posts or publications
- Mutual connections
- Career history (relevant highlights)

**Conversation Intelligence:**
- Their likely pain points (based on industry and company stage)
- Questions to ask (tailored to their situation)
- Relevant case studies from your portfolio
- Potential objections and responses
- Talking points that connect their needs to your solutions

**Context from Your Systems:**
- Previous interactions (emails, calls, meetings)
- CRM notes and deal history
- Any open proposals or quotes
- Last touchpoint and what was discussed

**Logistics:**
- Meeting time and duration
- Video link or location
- Attendee list with roles
- Your meeting objective (close, qualify, follow up, etc.)

## Building Your AI Meeting Prep System

### Architecture Overview

```
Calendar event (trigger)
    ↓
Extract attendee information
    ↓
AI researches company + person
    ↓
Pull CRM history and context
    ↓
AI generates briefing document
    ↓
Deliver to you (email, Slack, or app)
    ↓
[After meeting]
    ↓
AI transcribes and summarizes
    ↓
Action items extracted and assigned
    ↓
CRM updated automatically
```

### Step 1: Calendar Integration

Connect to your Google Calendar or Outlook:

- Trigger fires 60 minutes before each external meeting
- Extracts: attendee names, email addresses, company (from email domain), meeting title

### Step 2: Research Layer

AI performs automated research:

**Company research:**
- Website scraping (about page, recent blog posts, product info)
- LinkedIn company page (size, industry, growth signals)
- News search (recent press, funding, executive changes)
- Tech stack detection (what tools they use)
- Reviews/reputation (G2, Glassdoor, Trustpilot)

**Person research:**
- LinkedIn profile (title, history, recent activity)
- Recent social media posts or articles
- Mutual connections
- Previous interactions in your CRM

### Step 3: CRM Context Pull

Automatically retrieve from your CRM:

- All previous emails and notes
- Deal stage and history
- Previous meeting summaries
- Proposals sent
- Support tickets (if existing client)
- Account value and engagement score

### Step 4: AI Briefing Generation

Feed all research + CRM data to AI with this framework:

```
Generate a meeting briefing for [Your Name]'s call with [Contact Name] 
from [Company Name].

Include:
1. Company snapshot (what they do, size, recent news)
2. Person snapshot (role, background, interests)
3. Likely pain points based on their industry and company stage
4. 3-5 tailored questions to ask
5. Relevant case study or success story to mention
6. Potential objections and recommended responses
7. Recommended meeting objective and ask
8. Previous interaction summary (if any)

Keep it concise — scannable in 2 minutes. Use bullet points.
```

### Step 5: Delivery

Choose your preferred delivery method:

- **Email:** Briefing sent to your inbox 60 minutes before the meeting
- **Slack/Teams:** Posted in a dedicated channel
- **Dashboard:** Available in a web app or mobile app
- **Calendar attachment:** Added directly to the calendar event

### Step 6: Post-Meeting Automation

After the meeting:

- **AI transcribes** the conversation (using Zoom, Fireflies, or Otter)
- **AI generates a summary** with key points and decisions
- **AI extracts action items** and assigns them
- **Follow-up email drafted** by AI based on the conversation
- **CRM updated** with meeting notes, next steps, and deal stage
- **Tasks created** in your project management tool

## The Complete Meeting Intelligence Loop

Your AI meeting prep system creates a complete intelligence loop:

1. **Before:** You're prepared with research and context
2. **During:** Conversation is transcribed and analyzed
3. **After:** Summary, action items, and follow-up are automated
4. **Over time:** AI learns what prep is most valuable to you and improves

Each meeting feeds data back into the system, making future preparations smarter.
